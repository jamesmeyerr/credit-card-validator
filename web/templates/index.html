Credit Card Validator

Validate credit card numbers with Luhn algorithm

Supported Card Networks

  • Visa (starts with 4)
  • Mastercard (starts with 51-55 or 2221-2720)
  • American Express (starts with 34 or 37)
  • Discover (starts with 6011, 644-649, 65, etc.)
  • JCB, UnionPay, Diners Club, RuPay, Maestro

Note: American Express requires a 4-digit CVV, all other cards use a 3-digit CVV.

Credit Card Validator - Built with Go, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS
